Open Source Software

I currently work at Indeed.com where I'm helping develop the open source software program. At Indeed, open source technologies are a big part of the digital infrastructure we use to help people get jobs. Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) is at the core of everything we do. The products we build realize significant improvements when we use software that is collaboratively built and maintained by the FOSS Community. By leveraging their talents, we increase our velocity and are able to maintain our infrastructure in union with many of the best developers around the world. This means we help people get jobs faster than we would if we decided to write every piece of software ourselves.
